Elote

Description

This traditional central and south american dish is the perfect sweet and spicy side. Pair it with a reverse seared steak and lemon-lime soda for a complete delicious meal

Ingredients

  • Unpeeled sweet corn cobs
  • Your favorite mayonaise
  • Ground paprika
  • Ground cayenne

Steps

  1. Preheat oven for 400 degrees
  2. Place corn cobs directly on middle rack and cook for 30 minutes
  3. Remove corn cobs and peal
  4. Slather with mayo
  5. Season with paprika and cayenne to taste
