Lemon Lime Soda

Description

This crisp and refreshing healthy soda is the perfect beverage to enjoy with your reverse seared steak

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 lime
  • 1 can of your favorite unflavored sparkling water
  • Ice, straw, and mason jar

Steps

  1. Juice lemon and lime into mason jar
  2. Add ice
  3. Fill with sparkling water
  4. Insert straw, stir, and enjoy
